# 🚗 Automotive Market Analysis

## 📌 Project Goal
The goal of this project is to analyze 140K+ Indian used car listings to understand pricing trends, demand hotspots, fuel and transmission preferences, and factors influencing resale value, helping uncover business insights from the automotive resale market.

---

## 📌 Business Problem
The business wants to answer:

- Which brands dominate used car sales?
- Which states contribute the highest demand?
- How do kilometers driven impact resale price?
- Which fuel and transmission combinations dominate the market?
- What are the yearly sales growth trends?

---

## 📂 Dataset
The dataset contains 140,904 Indian used car listings with attributes including:

- Brand
- Model Name
- Variant
- Car Type
- Fuel Type
- Transmission
- Manufacturing Year
- Kilometers Driven
- Seller Type
- State
- Selling Price

This project uses a large-scale used car market dataset from Kaggle.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Excel → Initial data exploration and validation
- Tableau → Dashboard storytelling and advanced visualizations
- Kaggle Dataset → Raw data source
- GitHub → Documentation and portfolio showcase

---

## 🔄 Workflow
1. Imported 140K+ used car listing data
2. Performed data validation and profiling
3. Created business KPIs:
   - Total Sales
   - Total Brands
   - Total Revenue
   - Average Kilometers
4. Built 3 Tableau dashboards:
   - Sales Overview
   - Market Exploration
   - Pricing & Transmission Analysis
5. Created advanced visual storytelling using:
   - Sankey chart
   - Bubble charts
   - Donut charts
   - Histograms
   - Scatter plots
6. Added navigation buttons and interactive metric views

---

## 📊 KPI Metrics
- Total Listings
- Total Brands
- Total Revenue
- Average Kilometers
- Fuel Type Share
- Transmission Share
- State-wise Demand
- Median Price
- Yearly Growth

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Maruti Suzuki dominates the used car market, followed by Hyundai
- Petrol vehicles contribute the largest share of listings
- Manual transmission dominates overall demand
- Maharashtra, Delhi, and Karnataka show strong regional demand
- Higher kilometers driven negatively impacts resale price
- Market listings show strong yearly growth in recent years

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Focus inventory acquisition in high-demand states
- Prioritize petrol and manual transmission stock
- Use kilometer-based pricing adjustments for valuation
- Increase premium pricing for low-kilometer vehicles
- Build state-specific resale strategies
